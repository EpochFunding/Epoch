# Funding Regimes

Epoch tracks funding dislocations, so it helps to bucket conditions into regimes rather than reacting to every print.

## Balanced

Funding is near normal and crowding signals do not justify extra attention.

## Stretched

Funding is extended enough that carry and positioning deserve monitoring, but a reversal is not yet urgent.

## Extreme

Funding, positioning, and liquidity all point to a crowded state where flips and squeezes become plausible.

## Reset

The prior extreme has unwound and the engine should avoid treating the old regime as still active.
